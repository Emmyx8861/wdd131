Website Planning Document

Project Site Plan by Emmanuel Adewemimo

Site Name

Name: The Hybrid Portfolio

Reason: This name reflects the dual nature of my website. It represents the "hybrid" combination of my practical professional achievements (including my startup experience) and my creative storytelling as an author.

Optional Domain: hybrid-portfolio.com

Site Purpose

This website serves as a unified digital platform split into three core pages (Home, Professional Experience, and Book Showcase). Its primary purpose is to act as an interactive resume for professional endeavors while providing a dedicated creative space to highlight the lore, characters, and plot overviews of the two books I am currently writing.

Scenarios

Target audience questions that drive the site's content:

  • What practical professional experience and startup background does the author have?
  • What are the plots of the two upcoming books, and who are the main characters?
  • How can I navigate between their professional resume and their creative writing projects?

Color Schema

The site utilizes a professional yet creative palette:

  • Deep Blue (#1A365D): Primary color. Used for headings, headers, and footer backgrounds to convey professionalism.
  • Off-White (#F7FAFC): Background color. Used for the main body background to keep the reading experience clean.
  • Dark Slate (#2D3748): Text color. Used for all main paragraph/body text to ensure high contrast and readability.
  • Warm Gold (#D69E2E): Accent color. Used for links, buttons, and highlighting key text.

Typography

  • Merriweather (Serif): Used for all Headings (h1, h2, h3). This font gives a nod to the "book/author" side of the portfolio, offering a classic, literary feel.
  • Open Sans (Sans-Serif): Used for Body Text (paragraphs, lists). This is a clean, modern font that ensures the professional resume and long-form lore paragraphs are easy to read on any device.
